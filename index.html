Visit Siliguri

Enjoy culture and delicacies in this vibrant city close to Darjeeling hills.

Top 3 Places to Visit

Bengal Safari

Witness the majestic power and beauty of the Royal Bengal Tiger in its natural habitat, and experience a once-in-a-lifetime encounter with one of the world's most magnificent creatures

River Rafting

Get your adrenaline pumping and embrace the thrill of the wild as you navigate through rushing rapids and breathtaking scenery on an unforgettable river rafting adventure.

Kali Temple

Discover the spiritual serenity and awe-inspiring architecture of a sacred temple dedicated to the fierce and powerful goddess Kali.
